Experience (XP) - Leveling

Throughout Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic, your character and your companions gain experience points or XP. Leveling or leveling up is where your character gains a level. The unique feature of Knights of the Old Republic is that you can select auto-leveling or manual leveling. Auto-Leveling lets the computer to select skills and skills and force powers for you. For the more custom and your own in-tune character, use the manual leveling. When you leveled up, your avatar in the lower right hand corner changes to an orange arrow.

To level up:
  • Go to the Character Screen in the pause menu.
  • Select whether you want to auto level or manual level

XP is accurate by killing mobs and completing missions. Many missions and side missions have multiple ways to complete. Some of the ways offer more XP than the others. The harder to do, the more XP you get.

Characters

The path towards victory over Darth Malak is long and difficult. There are others in the galaxy willing to aid your journey. Your companions have their own motivations and backgrounds; you are able to control them any time. They are important to your success and each has their own skills, feats, and powers. Your party contains two other characters, while the others wait in your ship.

You can switch out characters in any safe location by choosing Party Selection from the Map Screen.

Bastila

Jedi Sentinel
Found: Taris

Valuable ally for the Republic and skilled with Battle Meditation, which led to victory over Dark Jedi Reven. Plenty of secrets and quite stubborn, but with her Double-Bladed Lightsaber she is a wiz. Kicking ass and taking names, strong in the Force, she is crucial to several plot points and is always wise to place her in your party.

Canderous

Soldier
Found: Taris

A Mandalorian bad-ass mercenary who believes honor is in short supply in the new era. This guy is looking for his next meal ticket. With his choice he chooses to tag along on your mission to save the galaxy from the Dark side. He can deal more than Carth and he is not a whining little baby. Non-Jedi characters that can deal with the baddies.

Carth

Soldier
Found:Endar Spire

A moody whiner, but a hero of the Mandalorian war; he cannot cope with his own failings and blames on himself. Not very trusting, but he has his reasons for his doubts. Excellent with ranged weapons, with a gun he is the man can do it and great support from the rear. When, more members join in he becomes much less valuable.

HK-47

Combat Droid
Found:Tatooine

An evil combat droid kept in storage for a long time and to eager to kill again; his loyalties to his master, but for the lust for bloodshed. Secrets lock inside this droid, secrets he will not reveal. The best thing is the entire game. Another great character to be in your party with his attitude and his firepower.

Jolee Bindo

Jedi Consular
Found:Kashyyyk

A Jedi anarchist of sorts and has given up of the Jedi Order and finds a balance between the Light and the Dark side. Every bit equal of Bastila in terms of power and combat skill.

Juhani

She has been on the Dark, where she attacked her master out of rage and consumed with hate. With her choice to join the Dark side, she can still be convinced to return to the Jedi Order. She will join your merry men through this journey to make amends for her naughty ways.

Instant Healing - In a safe location, you can jump back to your hideout/ ship and heal and then you can jump back to the location came from. A great way to save your med packs.

Art of Conversation – There is a lot of NPC that you can talk to for selling items to help on your main quest to side quests. You are given multiple responses and how you respond can directly effect on the game. For many tasks and many conversations; there are Light, Dark, and a neutral responses.

Being Good – Often times the bad response will sometimes lead to a fight. Being nice can open up new side quests. Some quests will only open to Dark Side character, but most are neutral or Light side responses.
